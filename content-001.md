## Process Section

### How Engagements Work

#### 1. Show me the problem

Bring the idea, workflow, prototype, system, or mess. It does not need to be polished.

#### 2. We clarify the goal

We identify what actually needs to be built, fixed, connected, or evaluated.

#### 3. I recommend a practical path

You get a clear technical direction, including tradeoffs, risks, and the next useful step.

#### 4. We build or improve the system

Depending on the project, that may mean a prototype, an internal tool, an integration, an AI feature, a rescue plan, or a full application.

#### 5. You leave with something usable

The goal is not complexity. The goal is software that makes the work simpler.
